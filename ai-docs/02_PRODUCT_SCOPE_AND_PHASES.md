# 02 — Product Scope and Phases

## Phase 1 — Manual MVP

Goal:
Launch a real comparison and lead generation platform with admin-managed operations.

Includes:
- Public SEO pages.
- Search and comparison pages.
- Lead form.
- Real database.
- Admin dashboard.
- Company/branch/car/offer management by admin.
- Manual lead assignment.
- Copy/Open WhatsApp message from lead data.
- Company follow-up.
- Customer follow-up.
- Activity log with date/time.

Excludes:
- Direct booking.
- Payment.
- Customer accounts.
- Full company dashboard.
- Automatic WhatsApp sending.
- OTP.
- Mobile app.

## Phase 1.5 — Strong Operations Layer

Goal:
Make manual operations efficient and measurable.

Includes:
- Duplicate lead detection.
- Lead intent score.
- Internal company quality score.
- Price freshness.
- Offer completeness score.
- Follow-up reminders.
- CSV export.
- Basic reports.
- UTM tracking.
- Social campaign attribution.

## Phase 2 — Company Dashboard

Goal:
Allow companies to manage their own data and leads.

Includes:
- Company login.
- Company roles.
- Manage company profile.
- Manage branches.
- Manage cars.
- Manage offers/prices.
- View assigned leads.
- Update lead status.
- Add company notes.
- Approval workflow based on trust level.
- Partner performance dashboard.

## Phase 2.5 — Automation

Goal:
Automate repetitive operations after manual process is proven.

Includes:
- n8n workflows.
- WhatsApp Business API.
- Auto-routing rules.
- Follow-up automation.
- No-response alerts.
- Weekly reports.
- AI-assisted lead matching.

## Phase 3 — Social Media Lead Engine

Goal:
Generate leads from social media, not only SEO.

Includes:
- Content calendar.
- AI-assisted post ideas.
- AI visual prompts.
- Scheduled posts.
- UTM tracking.
- Social campaign reporting.
- Landing pages by campaign.

## Phase 4 — Mobile App / PWA

Goal:
Improve user convenience and retention.

Includes:
- PWA first.
- Mobile app later.
- Saved searches.
- Push notifications.
- Feedback flow.

## Phase 5 — Booking and Payment

Future only.

Only consider this after:
- Legal review.
- Company agreements.
- Real availability.
- Cancellation/refund policies.
- Payment provider integration.
- Customer support process.
- Settlement process with companies.
