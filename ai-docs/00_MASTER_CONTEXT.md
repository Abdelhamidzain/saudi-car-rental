# 00 — Master Context

## Project Name

Working name: Saudi Car Rental Platform

Possible public positioning:
- Saudi Car Rental Comparison Platform
- منصة مقارنة تأجير السيارات في السعودية
- Car rental offers comparison and lead generation platform

## Main Goal

Build one of the strongest car rental comparison platforms in Saudi Arabia.

The platform should help customers:
- Search car rental offers.
- Compare prices, companies, cities, airports, car models, and rental conditions.
- Submit a rental request with their phone number and search details.
- Let the selected or most suitable rental company contact them directly.

The platform should help rental companies:
- Receive qualified leads.
- Manage their profile, branches, cars, offers, and prices in a later phase.
- Track lead performance through a company dashboard later.
- Grow their visibility through SEO and eventually social media lead generation.

## Current MVP Position

The MVP is manual.

Current intended MVP workflow:
1. Customer searches/compares.
2. Customer submits lead form.
3. Lead is saved in database.
4. Admin reviews the lead.
5. Admin assigns the lead to selected or suitable company.
6. Admin uses generated WhatsApp message from dashboard.
7. Admin sends it manually to the company.
8. Company contacts the customer directly.
9. Admin follows up with company and customer.
10. All actions are logged with date and time.

The platform can evolve into:
- A trusted comparison platform.
- A lead generation marketplace.
- A self-service SaaS dashboard for car rental companies.
- A social media lead engine.
- A mobile app.
- A future booking/payment platform only when legally and operationally ready.

## Founder Intent

The founder wants the project to start lean and manual, then gradually automate.

The founder prefers:
- Clear structured systems.
- Admin control first.
- Manual WhatsApp routing first.
- n8n/AI automation later.
- Arabic customer-facing platform at first.
- English documentation for Claude/LLM efficiency.
