## Core Product Identity

The platform is a Saudi car rental comparison and lead generation platform.

It is not a booking platform in the MVP.

It does not process payments in the MVP.

It does not rent cars directly.

The customer searches, compares, and submits a request.

The rental company contacts the customer directly.
