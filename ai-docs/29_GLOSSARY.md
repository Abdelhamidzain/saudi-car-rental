# 29 — Glossary

## Lead

A customer request submitted through the platform.

## Selected Offer Lead

A lead where the customer selected a specific company/offer.

## Best Offer Lead

A lead where the customer asks the platform to route the request to the best suitable company.

## Offer

A company-specific rental price/condition for a specific car in a specific city/branch.

## Starts-from Price

Indicative starting price, not final guaranteed price.

## Company Quality Score

Internal score used for ranking and routing. Not shown publicly.

## Price Freshness

How recently an offer price was updated.

## Activity Log

Timeline of all lead actions with date and time.

## Company Follow-up

Admin/company-side tracking of whether the company responded or contacted customer.

## Customer Follow-up

Admin tracking of the customer's real experience after lead routing.

## Trust Level

Internal operational status of company:
- new_partner
- verified_partner
- trusted_partner
- auto_approved_partner
- blocked

## Public Badge

Positive label shown to customers, such as Verified or Fast Response.

## Admin Dashboard

Internal dashboard for the platform owner/team.

## Company Dashboard

Future dashboard for rental companies to manage data and leads.
