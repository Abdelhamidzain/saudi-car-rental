# 03 — Business Model

## MVP Business Model

No monetization required at the beginning.

Phase 1 goal:
- Build traffic.
- Prove customer demand.
- Collect leads.
- Test company responsiveness.
- Build relationships with rental companies.
- Measure lead quality.

## Future Monetization Options

Recommended order:

### 1. Subscription Plans

Companies pay monthly to:
- Appear on platform.
- Manage profile.
- Receive leads.
- Access dashboard insights.
- Add more offers/branches.

Possible plans:
- Basic
- Pro
- Featured
- Enterprise

### 2. Lead Fee

Company pays per qualified lead.

Lead must be defined clearly:
- Valid Saudi phone.
- Clear city.
- Rental date.
- Car category or selected offer.
- Customer consent.

### 3. Featured Listings

Companies pay for higher visibility.

Important:
- Any paid placement should be clearly marked as featured/sponsored where appropriate.
- Internal quality score should still prevent bad companies from dominating.

### 4. Commission

Not recommended early.

Hard to track unless:
- Platform controls booking.
- Company confirms deal.
- Customer confirms deal.
- There is reliable reporting.

### 5. Booking/Payment Commission

Future only.

Requires legal, operational, payment, refund, cancellation, and support readiness.

## Value Proposition to Companies

The platform gives companies:
- Qualified leads.
- SEO visibility.
- Organized requests.
- Customer search intent.
- Dashboard analytics.
- Performance reporting.
- Future social media exposure.

## Value Proposition to Customers

The platform gives customers:
- Easier comparison.
- Faster access to rental companies.
- Better price visibility.
- More transparency.
- Ability to request best available offer.
