# 23 — Partner Onboarding and Verification

## Purpose

Define how rental companies join the platform in future.

## MVP

Companies are added manually by admin.

No public claim page in MVP unless explicitly requested.

## Future Partner Onboarding

Company submits:
- Company name.
- Commercial registration if needed.
- Main contact person.
- Official phone.
- Official WhatsApp.
- Website.
- Google Maps links.
- Branches.
- Cars/offers.
- Logo.

## Verification Levels

### new_partner
Newly added, needs admin review.

### verified_partner
Basic data verified.

### trusted_partner
Good response and customer feedback.

### auto_approved_partner
Can publish some updates without review.

### blocked
Hidden from public and cannot receive leads.

## Verification Checklist

- Company name verified.
- Phone/WhatsApp verified.
- Branch exists.
- Google Maps link checked.
- Offers make sense.
- No repeated customer complaints.
- Responds to leads.

## Public Badges

Show only positive badges:
- Verified
- Fast response
- Updated prices
- Airport delivery
- Monthly rental options

Do not show internal trust level publicly.
