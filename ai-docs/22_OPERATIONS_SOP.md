# 22 — Operations SOP

## Purpose

Daily operating procedure for manual MVP.

## Daily Admin Routine

1. Open admin dashboard.
2. Review new leads.
3. Check duplicate warnings.
4. Assign leads to companies.
5. Copy/Open WhatsApp messages.
6. Send messages manually.
7. Update lead status.
8. Follow up with pending companies.
9. Follow up with selected customers.
10. Close leads as won/lost/spam.
11. Add notes for any issue.

## New Lead SOP

When a new lead arrives:

1. Check customer phone.
2. Check city/date/category.
3. Check selected offer or best offer.
4. Check if duplicate.
5. Assign company:
   - selected company if specific offer
   - best suitable company if best offer
6. Generate WhatsApp message.
7. Send manually.
8. Mark as sent_to_company.
9. Schedule customer follow-up.

## Company Follow-up SOP

If company has not responded:
- Follow up after suitable time.
- Mark no_response if needed.
- Reassign to another company if urgent.

## Customer Follow-up SOP

Ask:
1. Did the company contact you?
2. Was price close to what you expected?
3. Did you rent the car?
4. How was the experience?

Record:
- customer outcome
- price match status
- customer rating
- notes

## Bad Experience SOP

If customer reports bad experience:
1. Record complaint.
2. Mark company issue.
3. Lower internal company score.
4. Review company visibility if repeated.

## Price Mismatch SOP

If customer says price changed:
1. Record price mismatch.
2. Update offer status to needs_confirmation.
3. Ask company to update price.
4. Lower ranking until price is updated.

## No Response Company SOP

If company repeatedly ignores leads:
1. Mark no response events.
2. Lower internal company score.
3. Remove from top recommendations.
4. Consider hiding or blocking.
