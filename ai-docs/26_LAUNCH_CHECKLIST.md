# 26 — Launch Checklist

## Before MVP Launch

### Product

- Public pages ready.
- Lead form ready.
- Consent text added.
- Disclaimer added.
- Privacy policy added.
- Terms added.
- No booking/payment wording.

### Data

- Priority cities added.
- Companies added.
- Branches added.
- WhatsApp numbers added.
- Cars added.
- Offers added.
- Prices set as starts-from.
- Last updated dates added.

### Admin

- Admin dashboard works.
- Lead list works.
- Lead detail works.
- Assign company works.
- Copy/Open WhatsApp works.
- Company follow-up works.
- Customer follow-up works.
- Activity log works.

### Analytics

- GA4 installed.
- Search Console configured.
- Clarity installed.
- UTM tracking works.
- Internal event tracking works.

### SEO

- Sitemap generated.
- Robots configured.
- Metadata checked.
- Schema checked.
- Internal links checked.

### Security

- Admin protected.
- Environment variables set.
- Lead form rate-limited.
- Server validation.
- Customer data not public.

### Operations

- Manual lead SOP ready.
- WhatsApp templates ready.
- Follow-up routine ready.
- Company response tracking ready.

## Soft Launch

Start with:
- Riyadh
- Jeddah
- Dammam

Track:
- Leads.
- Company response.
- Price mismatch.
- Customer feedback.
- Best performing pages.

## Do Not Launch If

- Leads are not saved.
- Admin cannot route leads.
- Customer data is exposed.
- Prices look guaranteed.
- No privacy/consent text.
- No activity logs.
